Turn Claude Into Your Full-Time Content Team

Source ↗ Content

What This Skill Is

Four content workflows that actually move the needle, from Modern Millie's tutorial:

  1. Free profile audit: Install the Claude Chrome extension, open your Instagram or YouTube Studio analytics, then ask Claude to audit your profile — it reads the live page and gives you hook strength, content gaps, and growth opportunities
  2. 30-day content calendar: Load a Claude Project with your brand info and use the prompt below
  3. Full video script: Pick a day from the calendar, then turn it into a script with text hook, verbal hook, visual hook, talking points, and CTA
  4. One video → one week: Get your YouTube transcript (transcript.io), start a new Project, and turn it into short-form scripts, captions, carousel outlines, and an email newsletter

Why It Works

Projects give Claude persistent memory of your brand, so you don't re-explain your niche every session. The calendar → script → repurpose pipeline turns one piece of effort into a week of content.

How to Use It

  1. Create a Claude Project and load it with your brand info (niche, audience, tone, goals)
  2. Run the calendar prompt above
  3. Pick days that look good and script them one at a time
  4. For existing videos: drop the transcript in and let Claude repurpose it across formats

Prompt Template

Based on everything you know about my brand and goals, build me a 30-day content calendar. Alternate between my content pillars and include 3 hook options per video.

---

Let's work on Day [X]. Use Hook [#]. Turn this into a full script with text hook, verbal hook, visual hook, talking points, and CTA.

---

Here's my transcript. Turn this into a full week of content: short-form scripts, captions, a carousel outline, and an email newsletter.

[paste transcript]
