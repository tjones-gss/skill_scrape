Hire Claude a Department

Source ↗ June 15, 2026 Content

What This Skill Is

Use Anthropic's knowledge work plugins to turn Claude into role-based specialists — sales, marketing, finance, legal, data, product, support, and more. Each plugin gives Claude the domain skills, slash commands, and tool connections that role needs, so you get a specialist rather than a generalist.

Why It Works

Most people use Claude like a freelancer with amnesia: one task, one answer, then back to zero. Role-based plugins give Claude persistent domain context, vocabulary, and tooling. Starting with one "hire" and connecting the right tools makes the AI feel like a real team member, not just a chat interface.

How to Use It

  1. Download Claude Desktop and open Cowork
  2. Add the plugin marketplace: claude plugin marketplace add anthropics/knowledge-work-plugins
  3. Install one role first: claude plugin install sales@knowledge-work-plugins
  4. Swap "sales" for: marketing, finance, legal, data, product-management, customer-support, or productivity
  5. Try a slash command: /sales:call-prep, /data:write-query, or /marketing:seo-audit
  6. Connect the tools that role needs (CRM, analytics, data warehouse, docs)
  7. Customize with your company's terminology and process

Prompt Template

I need you to act as my [role] specialist.

My company context: [describe your company, industry, and size]
The tasks I need this specialist to handle: [list 3-5 recurring tasks]
Tools and systems I can connect: [list apps, CRMs, databases, etc.]
Domain vocabulary to know: [any internal terms, product names, processes]

Set up your approach as a senior [role] professional for this context. Start by asking me what task to tackle first, and tell me which slash commands or workflows will be most useful for me to learn.
